What is a Landslide?

When soil, rock, and other earth debris can no longer hold it together and gives way to gravity, landslides happen.The downward force of a landslide can move slowly, (a mere millimeters per year) or quickly with disasterous effects. Landslides can even occur underwater, causing tidal waves and damage to coastal areas. These landslides are called submarine landslides.
